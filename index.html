About This Project

We all know that electrical energy is of great importance for scientific advancement and life in general. And man has sought to obtain it since it was discovered. But something happened that is environmental pollution. The old methods of producing energy caused great and dangerous pollution on our earth. And from that point, people started thinking about alternative (clean) energy. My project Discusses the danger of pollution caused by the production of energy.
